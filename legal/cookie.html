What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored on your browser or device. Similar technologies may include pixels, tags, local storage, scripts, and analytics identifiers.

Cookie 是存储在浏览器或设备上的小文件。类似技术可能包括像素、标签、本地存储、脚本和分析标识符。

How We May Use Cookies

We may use cookies to operate the site, remember preferences, understand website traffic, measure performance, improve user experience, protect security, and support marketing analytics where permitted.

我们可能使用 Cookie 来运行网站、记住偏好、了解访问流量、衡量性能、改善体验、保护安全，并在允许范围内支持营销分析。

Third-Party Cookies

Third-party providers, such as analytics, hosting, embedded media, advertising, CRM, form, or automation tools, may set their own cookies. Their use of cookies is controlled by their own policies.

分析、托管、嵌入媒体、广告、CRM、表单或自动化工具等第三方服务可能设置自己的 Cookie，其使用方式受其自身政策约束。

Your Choices

You can usually block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Some site features may not work correctly if cookies are disabled. Where required, we will request consent before using non-essential cookies.

你通常可以通过浏览器设置阻止或删除 Cookie。禁用 Cookie 可能导致部分网站功能无法正常使用。在法律要求的情况下，我们会在使用非必要 Cookie 前征得同意。
