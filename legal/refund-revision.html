Refund / Revision Policy

This Policy explains how Dodva Studio handles project payments, revisions, approvals, and refund requests.

本政策说明 Dodva Studio 如何处理项目付款、修改、确认与退款请求。

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Project Scope Controls

Each project should have a written scope, proposal, invoice, or agreement describing deliverables, revision rounds, timeline, fees, and responsibilities. Work outside the agreed scope may require a new quote or change order.

每个项目应有书面范围、方案、账单或协议，说明交付内容、修改轮次、时间、费用与责任。超出约定范围的工作可能需要新的报价或变更确认。

Revisions

Revision rounds are intended to refine approved project direction, not to create a completely new concept or replace the original scope. Delayed feedback, new goals, new assets, or major strategic changes may affect timeline and pricing.

修改轮次用于完善已确认的项目方向，而不是重新创建全新概念或替代原始范围。反馈延迟、新目标、新素材或重大策略变化可能影响时间和费用。

Deposits and Payments

Deposits, retainers, rush fees, completed work, strategy work, consulting, research, AI exploration, and approved milestones are generally non-refundable unless a written agreement says otherwise.

除非书面协议另有约定，定金、预付款、加急费、已完成工作、策略工作、咨询、研究、AI 探索和已确认阶段通常不可退款。

Refund Requests

Refund requests are reviewed case by case. We may decline refunds for completed work, client delays, change of mind, unused approved deliverables, third-party platform issues, or outcomes outside Dodva Studio’s control.

退款请求将逐案审核。对于已完成工作、客户延迟、主观改变、未使用但已批准的交付物、第三方平台问题或 Dodva Studio 无法控制的结果，我们可能拒绝退款。

Final Approval

Once you approve deliverables for launch, publishing, export, transfer, or commercial use, you accept responsibility for final review, accuracy, compliance, and use.

一旦你批准交付物用于上线、发布、导出、转移或商业使用，即表示你对最终审核、准确性、合规性和使用承担责任。
